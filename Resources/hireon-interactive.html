Phase 01 — Intake
AI Resume Intelligence
The moment a candidate uploads their resume, Aura's AI engine instantly begins a multi-layer analysis — no waiting, no queue.
Aura processes PDF and DOCX files in parallel. It doesn't just extract text — it understands context. A candidate who lists "built e-commerce checkout flows" gets Redux and REST APIs inferred as hidden skills, even if they didn't explicitly write them.
📄
Document Parsing
PDF, DOCX, and plain text resumes extracted with layout-aware parsing. Handles columns, tables, and non-standard templates.

🏷️
Explicit Skill Extraction

Hidden Skill Inference
Reads between the lines. "Led API integration for 3 microservices" → infers REST, Git, Postman even if not listed explicitly.
Sonnet 4.5
📊
Experience Calculation
Parses dates from all positions, handles gaps, overlaps, and freelance work. Returns precise decimal years (e.g. 6.5y).

Phase 02 — Scoring
Smart Auto-Shortlisting
Every candidate is scored against your exact role requirements. Those who clear your threshold are instantly shortlisted — automatically.
You set the rules once. Aura enforces them perfectly, every time, at scale. A match score of 87% against an 80% threshold means the candidate is shortlisted, their status updated, your HR team notified in the dashboard, and the candidate emailed — all within seconds of application.
🎯
Match Score Calculation
Weighted comparison of candidate skills, experience, and seniority vs. role requirements. Returns a 0–100% score.
Sonnet 4.5
⚡

Phase 03 — Scheduling
Conflict-Free Scheduling
One click. Aura cross-references calendars, blocks slots, generates a Meet link, and sends invites to everyone — in 30 seconds.
What used to take 15 emails and 3 days of back-and-forth now takes a single button press. Aura fetches both the candidate's submitted availability and the interviewer's live calendar, finds the overlap, and handles everything — including preventing double-bookings across multiple concurrent hiring pipelines.
📆
Availability Cross-Reference
Fetches candidate's submitted time windows and interviewer's Google/Outlook calendar. Finds optimal overlapping slot.
Automated

Phase 04 — Decision
Interview Intelligence & Hiring
Post-interview AI analysis turns structured feedback into a hiring recommendation — with an updated probability score and permanent candidate storage.
The interviewer submits a structured feedback form rating Technical Skills, Communication, and Culture Fit with a Hire / No Hire / Maybe recommendation. Aura's AI synthesizes this, updates the hire probability (e.g. 75% → 82%), generates a summary, and surfaces it all in the HR dashboard. Every candidate — hired or not — is permanently stored and re-matchable.
📝
Structured Feedback Collection
