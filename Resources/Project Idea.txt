4. Core Product Features
1. AI Resume Intelligence Engine

When a candidate applies:

The system generates:

Experience calculation

Skill extraction (explicit + inferred)

Seniority level

Strength/weakness analysis

Match score vs job requirement

Hire probability score

Result:

HR sees ranked candidates instantly.

2. Smart Requirement Matching

Each job role has:

Required tech stack

Experience threshold

Match score threshold

If AI score meets threshold:

Candidate is automatically shortlisted.

No manual filtering required.

3. Automated Scheduling Engine

The system:

Matches candidate availability with interviewer availability

Prevents slot collision

Books interviews automatically

Generates Google Meet links

Sends calendar invites

Scheduling time reduced from hours → seconds.

4. Interview Intelligence

After interview:

Interviewer submits feedback

AI summarizes feedback

AI updates hire probability

HR sees structured recommendation

5. Permanent Talent Database (Key Differentiator)

This builds a:

Company-Owned AI Talent Repository

Over time, hiring becomes faster and more intelligent.
